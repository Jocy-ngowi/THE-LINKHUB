About Us

The Link Hub Africa is a youth-focused empowerment and business facilitation company committed to bridging the gap between education, industry, and entrepreneurship.

Our Approach

We provide practical solutions that prepare young people for the workforce, support SMEs, and help corporates implement impactful CSR initiatives. Through strategic partnerships with employers, government institutions, development partners, and educational institutions, we create opportunities that enhance employability, entrepreneurship, and sustainable business growth.

Services overview

  • Facilitation Program (internships, placements, mentorship)
  • Training & Certification Programs
  • CSR Consulting & Advisory
  • SME Support Packages
  • Study Abroad & Exchange Programs
  • Technology & Digital Solutions
  • Marketing, Communication & Media
